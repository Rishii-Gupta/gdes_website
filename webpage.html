OUR GAMES

WIKSON THE IGNITER

2D Puzzle Platformer where players control Wickson, a matchbox, in the paper-made world of Papyrusburg. By flicking matches, players ignite fires that spread across the environment, interacting with paper and wood elements.

ROBOT GALAXY

3D sci-fi exploration and third person shooter game set in a futuristic universe where you take on the role of an advanced robot, programmed to eliminate the remaining human resistance.

TWIG TO TREE

Help Groot find his partner Rocket and defeat the evil Bounty Hunter. Experience a new story in a new world. Explore the dangerous platforms. Unlock abilities to overcome challenges.
